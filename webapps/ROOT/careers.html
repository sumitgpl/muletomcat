Career At Bizgrass

How we hire

We are looking for our next team member. Someone who's is good for the role we are looking and good for Bizgrass.

We have started growing business so things move quickly around here. So we have to nimble on how we work and how we hire. We look for people who can work independently, dedicatedly with creativity and like to take challanges. We're looking for people who are good for Bizgrass, and not just for right now, but for the long term.


How we interview

We are looking for smart, talented, team oriented and people who can get things done. When we look for someone to join our team, we look for basically 4 qualitites:

Leadership

Create a difference

We would like to know how you have reacted in different problematic situations in order to keep the things going. This might be when you were in a leadership at work or at an organization level. This can be even by helping pears and team while you were not officially at leadership role.

We look for this quality as we believe you will not work for Bizgrass but you will work with Bizgrass.

Role Knowledge

Track the Strength

We are looking for people who have complete nuance of strength and passion, not just isolated ones. For engineering, masters in technology candidates particularly, we will be looking to check your coding style and skills, and your approach to handle a problem. You can also expect that we might check you on design patterns and algorithm design.

Thought process

An approach is all that matters

We do not look at your grades, or your college. We look for the capability in you. We believe in what you are not what you were. Its your current calibre which we look for. We're likely to ask you some role-related questions that provide insight into how you solve problems. Show us how you would tackle the problem presented

Craziness

We want to get a feel of what makes you feel at best. We also want to make sure, Bizgrass becomes the best place of work for you, a place where you will thrive. A place where you will have fun along with work so that the craziest ideas can come out of your mind and you can actualize them. Things which you think, make them happen in your life, in real.
