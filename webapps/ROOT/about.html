Bizgrass Team

Abhishek Bansal / Founder, CEO

As Bizgrass's chief executive officer, Abhishek is responsible for Bizgrass's day-to-day operations, as well as leading the company's product development and technology strategy. He primarily look into core algorithm designing for the various modules and their integration.

Abhishek is an innovative, creative technologist and Co-Founder at Bizgrass.He is very fond of sports, playing guitar and photography. He hold a bachelor's degree in engineering - Computer Science and a master's degree in business administration where he has been honored with many prizes over his technical and strategical skills.

Sumit Dubey / Founder, CIO

Sumit Dubey as Chief Information Officer in Bizgrass is responsible for Cloud Application Management, CRM, Accounting modules and database activities. He has worked on all types of database. He has expertise in financial, account and e-commerce domain with vast experience for several years.

Sumit has Masters in Computer Science where he expertise in research and implementation of enterprise applications. He also has two international research papers published on 'Rules extracting from PLSQL' and 'Framework for the SHELL'.

Shivangi Jain Mehra / Chief Designer

Shivangi Jain Mehra, a creative enthusiast, is Chief Designer at Bizgrass, where she manages the experience design and the brand identity.

She is an architect with immense talent in the realm of design, and has pursued a landscape architecture program from University of California at Berkeley. She started out designing buildings, and landscapes and then her passion of reading took her to designing books, e-books and then web experiences. Having worked with both large companies, as well as smaller entrepreneurial setups, she finds her creativity finds its best expression in the latter.

She likes to explore the world through her designs and learn something new at every step. Shivangi loves all these different expression of design and works with a number of organizations.

Sambhav is a computer science engineer with deep passion for software design and development using Java and web technologies stack. Being chief technology officer at Bizgrass, he makes sure that our continously evolving product is up to the market in terms of technogies, features and perfomance.
